Welcome Journey Automation

Salesforce Marketing Cloud Journey Builder Concept Project

Project Overview

This project demonstrates a customer onboarding automation workflow modeled after Salesforce Marketing Cloud Journey Builder.

The goal of the journey is to improve customer engagement, increase first purchases, and personalize onboarding communication based on subscriber behavior.

Business Goal

New subscribers were signing up but failing to engage after the initial email interaction. This journey was designed to increase engagement and move customers toward conversion through personalized lifecycle communication.

Segmentation Strategy

Subscriber data was organized using Data Extension concepts including:

  • Subscriber Key
  • First Name
  • Email Address
  • Purchase Status
  • Engagement Activity

Decision splits and segmentation logic were based on engagement behavior and purchase activity.
