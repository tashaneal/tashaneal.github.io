Household Journey Automation

Salesforce Marketing Cloud-Inspired Lifecycle Automation Concept

Project Overview

This project reframes a household automation system as a Salesforce Marketing Cloud-inspired lifecycle communication journey. It demonstrates how recurring family operations can be organized using journey logic, segmentation, triggered messages, and multi-channel communication.

Business Problem

Busy families often struggle with recurring routines, missed tasks, inconsistent communication, and mental load. This automation concept helps reduce friction by sending timely reminders, routing household needs, and organizing communication by audience and priority.

Message Examples

SMS Reminder: Good morning! School bags, lunch boxes, and shoes should be ready by 7:30 AM.

Email Summary: Today’s household priorities include school prep, dinner plan, laundry rotation, and one open family support ticket.

Support Ticket Alert: A high-priority household need has been submitted and assigned for follow-up.

Audience Segments

Parents

Receive summaries, escalations, and priority reminders.

Children

Receive age-appropriate checklist reminders and routine nudges.

Recurring Tasks

Scheduled journeys for chores, meals, appointments, and routines.

High-Priority Alerts

Triggered messages for urgent household needs or support tickets.
