Preference Center

Salesforce Marketing Cloud Subscriber Management Concept Project

Project Overview

This project demonstrates a subscriber preference center inspired by Salesforce Marketing Cloud CloudPages and subscriber management workflows.

The goal is to improve customer retention and engagement by allowing subscribers to customize communication preferences instead of fully unsubscribing.
